I've been playing bass for many years, studied Piano to A.B. Grade 8, and played lead guitar in several bands when I still had hair.
I have played a variety of other instruments along the way, including most varieties of guitar, things with a piano keyboard, larger stuff made of brass (with valves and slides), a bit of drumming, even the odd saxophone (badly).

Brotherhoods Roundabout, 2006-present

Various original and covers bands in Rotherham, 1986-1992. We used various names: The Dreaming, Lost Identity, Seventh Son. Betcha no-one remembers now...
